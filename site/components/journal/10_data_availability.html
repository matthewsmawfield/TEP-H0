Data Availability

The SH0ES 2022 distance moduli and covariance matrices are publicly available at github.com/PantheonPlusSH0ES/DataRelease. The Pantheon+ supernova compilation is available at the same repository. Velocity dispersion measurements were drawn from HyperLEDA, SDSS DR7, and published literature sources cited in Section 2.2. The complete analysis pipeline, processed host-galaxy catalogue (hosts_processed.csv), all output statistics, and figures are publicly available at github.com/matthewsmawfield/TEP-H0 and archived at doi:10.5281/zenodo.18209702 (CC BY 4.0). The pipeline automatically retrieves all third-party data from public archives and fully reproduces all results, figures, and statistics reported in this paper.
